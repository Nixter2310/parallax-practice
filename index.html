Who doesn't love a good hot chocolate?

Here's a simple recipe for some delicious hot chocolate, perfect for a chilly Winter's day!

Ingredients:

  • 250ml milk of your choice
  • 1 tbsp cocoa
  • 1-2 tbsp soft light brown sugar
  • 25g dark or plain chocolate, finely chopped, plus a grating to serve
  • 1 tbsp whipped cream or squirty cream

Method:

STEP 1: Heat the milk, cocoa, sugar and chocolate in a small pan over a medium heat until steaming and the chocolate has melted. Whisk to dissolve the cocoa.

STEP 2: Pour into a mug, then add the cream to float on top. Grate over a little more chocolate to serve.

STEP 3: Enjoy!
